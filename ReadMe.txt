Controls:

Keyboard:
Up Arrow - move up
Down Arrow - move down
Left Arrow - strafe left
Right Arrow - strafe right

w - turn on and off collision
z - zoom in
Z - zoom out
d - turn on or off day night
f - turn on and off fog
shift and arrows - sprint
c - crouch
home - reset position
move mouse - move camera
escape - quit game

GamePad:
Left anolog - move
right anolog - move camera
start - reset position
x - turn on and off fog
y - turn on and off collision
right bumper - turn on or off day night
b - zoom in
a - zoom out
left analog click - sprint
left bumper - crouch
back - quit game
